# Assumed Breach Methodology

## Overview

Assumed Breach assessments start from the premise that an attacker has already obtained initial access.

The focus shifts from gaining entry to evaluating the potential impact of compromise.

---

## Objectives

* Measure blast radius
* Identify attack paths
* Evaluate privilege escalation
* Assess detection capabilities

---

## Typical Workflow

1. Initial foothold
2. Enumeration
3. Credential discovery
4. Lateral movement
5. Privilege escalation
6. Tier 0 assessment
7. Reporting

---

## Assessment Focus

* Administrative exposure
* Session exposure
* Delegation
* Service accounts
* PKI infrastructure
