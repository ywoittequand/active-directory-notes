# Reporting Methodology

## Overview

Technical findings create value only when clearly communicated.

Effective reporting translates technical risk into business impact.

---

## Finding Structure

### Title

Clear and concise.

### Description

Explain the issue.

### Technical Details

Document evidence and attack path.

### Risk

Explain impact.

### Remediation

Provide actionable guidance.

---

## Risk Assessment

Consider:

* Exploitability
* Privilege required
* Business impact
* Exposure

---

## Executive Communication

Focus on:

* Critical risks
* Tier 0 exposure
* Business consequences
* Prioritization

---

## Reporting Goals

* Clarity
* Accuracy
* Actionability
* Reproducibility

---

## Common Mistakes

* Tool output without analysis
* Excessive technical detail
* Missing business context
* Weak remediation guidance
