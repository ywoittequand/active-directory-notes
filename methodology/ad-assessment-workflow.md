# Active Directory Assessment Workflow

## Phase 1 – Discovery

Identify:

* Domains
* Forests
* Trusts
* Administrative groups

---

## Phase 2 – Enumeration

Collect:

* Users
* Groups
* Computers
* ACLs
* Sessions

---

## Phase 3 – Attack Path Mapping

Analyze:

* BloodHound paths
* Delegation
* Service accounts
* Privilege relationships

---

## Phase 4 – Validation

Validate:

* Escalation opportunities
* Lateral movement paths
* Tier 0 exposure

---

## Phase 5 – Risk Evaluation

Determine:

* Likelihood
* Impact
* Exploitability

---

## Phase 6 – Reporting

Provide:

* Executive summary
* Technical findings
* Remediation guidance
